## RoomLight Controller - Bringing The Future to The Hotel Rooms of Today

Juho-ryhmä finally announced the release of the long-awaited RoomLight Controller platform, bringing modern technology to the well-established world of accommodation. Take hotel management to the next level without sacrificing the classic charm that comes with the industry.

Designing, setting up, and managing lighting in large hotels can be outright impossible in certain cases. However, RoomLight Controller takes away the hard parts and leaves you with the ultimate freedom of expression. 

“Modern software and AI programs have changed how many industries work. We wanted to change the world of hotels by tackling the issues of complicated lighting and unintuitive user experiences through the eyes of a company with its sights set on the far future.”

Setting up the platform is as easy as any other contemporary software. It allows for easy management through a simple, modern, and unobtrusive user interface that offers the finest user experience on the market.

“Our customers have absolutely loved the new lighting options in our hotel rooms,” said a manager at the Compact Hill Hotel.
